About the program

Architecture guidance with practical depth

Copilot Studio Architecture Bootcamp is a fast-paced 3-day intermediate-to-advanced program aligned to the Microsoft Copilot Studio Implementation Guidance, focused on proven patterns, de-risking complex projects, and under-the-hood architecture decisions.

Program update: The Copilot Studio Architecture Bootcamp has been re-launched with 16 modules and 10 hands-on labs, focused on architecture, governance, and real-world implementation patterns.

Microsoft employees are invited to attend virtually. Please note that travel and expenses are not provided.

We highly suggest attending in-person to meet with industry professionals and trainers.

Audience fit

Who this bootcamp is for

Designed for technical professionals who architect, deliver, or govern Copilot Studio solutions in enterprise environments.

Typical attendees

  • Solution and Enterprise Architects
  • Delivery Consultants and Implementation Leads
  • Partner Technical Architects and Customer Engineers
  • Technical Strategists overseeing architecture or governance

Best suited for participants who

  • Already have Power Platform or Copilot Studio experience
  • Own solution architecture, governance, or implementation strategy
  • Want deeper guidance on scalable patterns, security, and architecture decisions

Note: This program is not intended for beginners who are new to Power Platform or Copilot Studio.

Registration closes one week before each event. In-person seats are limited and subject to change based on demand.

Virtual events are open to Microsoft employees globally.
