About Copilot Studio Architecture Bootcamp

Power CAT is excited to announce new hybrid (in-person or virtual) sessions. This intensive 3-day training, aligned with Microsoft Copilot Studio (MCS) Implementation Guidance, will empower Solution Architects to master implementation patterns, de-risk complex projects, and apply the latest tech with confidence.

We highly suggest attending in-person to meet with industry professionals and trainers.

Who Should Attend?

Partners, Customers, and Microsoft internal roles (online only), including:

  • Architects
  • Technical Consultants/Delivery Leads

Registration closes one week before each event. In-person seats are limited and subject to change based on demand.
