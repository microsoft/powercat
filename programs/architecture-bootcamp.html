About Copilot Studio Architecture Bootcamp

This intensive 3-day training, aligned with the Microsoft Copilot Studio (MCS) Implementation Guidance, empowers Solution Architects to master implementation patterns, de-risk complex projects, and apply the latest tech with confidence.

We highly suggest attending in-person to meet with industry professionals and trainers.

Pre-Bootcamp Learning Resources

This Bootcamp is delivered at an intermediate level and does not focus on introductory Copilot Studio concepts during sessions. We highly recommend reviewing the following materials prior to attending the Copilot Studio Architecture Bootcamp. These resources are foundational and cover all UI elements and Level 100 concepts essential for success in the bootcamp.

Who Should Attend?

Technical practitioners who implement Copilot solutions end‑to‑end, including:

  • Architects
  • Technical Consultants/Delivery Leads

Registration closes one week before each event. In-person seats are limited and subject to change based on demand. Microsoft internal roles are welcome to attend virtual events.

Agenda At a Glance

  • Day 1 – What does success look like?
    Share product strategy, new product capabilities, and learnings from successful customer implementations.
  • Day 2 – How do I implement successfully?
    Provide education on CAT playbook (Copilot Studio Implementation Guide) to architect, develop, govern, and deploy better solutions.
  • Day 3 – What capabilities do I use to implement successfully?
    Share in-depth product capabilities (i.e., under-the-hood architectures) to make better-informed decisions based on patterns.
    Hands-on labs will be included.
