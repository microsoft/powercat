About the Webinar Series

Microsoft CAT AI Webinars are a series of interactive virtual sessions designed to help your organization adopt Microsoft Copilot and agents.

In these sessions you'll engage with Microsoft experts and other customers to gain the knowledge and skills needed to successfully adopt and thrive with agents in your organization.

What to Expect

  • Gain the tactical and strategic expertise needed to succeed with Microsoft Copilot and agents in your organization.
  • You'll explore the possibilities of Microsoft Copilot, upcoming advancements with autonomous agents, how to extend with Azure AI Foundry, and more.
  • Each workshop includes interactive exercises that allow you to engage and learn from others.
  • You'll benefit from Microsoft's expertise in helping customers successfully adopt AI.
