M365 Copilot Chat

Zero-effort AI inside Office

Personal productivity assistant for writing, data analysis, and information retrieval within M365 workflows.

Best For:
  • Individual productivity boost
  • Document creation & editing
  • Meeting summarization
  • Quick Office app insights
Lowest Complexity

Agents in Microsoft 365

Pre-built specialized agents

Purpose-built agents like Researcher, Analyst, Facilitator, and Employee Self-Service available in the Agent Store.

Best For:

  • Meeting facilitation & notes

  • Skills & expertise discovery

M365 Copilot + Agent Builder

Lightweight customization without code

Extends Copilot with organization-specific knowledge and simple automations while staying in M365.

Best For:
  • Internal FAQs & policies
  • Company-specific Q&A
  • Simple task automation
  • SharePoint/Graph integration
Low Complexity

Copilot Studio

Flexibility and reach with low-code

Custom conversational agents with workflow automation and broad enterprise integration capabilities.

Best For:
  • Custom departmental bots
  • Customer service agents
  • Process automation
  • Power Platform integration

Scalability and depth for enterprise

Enterprise AI/ML platform for bespoke solutions with full control over models and infrastructure.
